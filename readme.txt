Create a Facebook fan pages with the Facebook pop out tab. The Tab sits at the side, top, and bottom of your site and pops out to reveal a like box for your fan page.  

== Description ==  
This jQuery base Facebook plugin places a discreet Facebook tab to the side, top, and bottom of your page visitors can hover over to reveal a like box for your Facebook Fan Page.A great way to increase your Facebook fans and a stylish addition to any Wordpress website or Blog.The plugin comes with a full list of settings to style the plugin to fit your site.The settings include Width, Height, Color Scheme and Background Color.You can also choose to have the Facebook tab on the left or right of your pages and even change the color of the tab.   

== Installation ==  
1. Upload `cool-fb-likebox` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory 
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress 3. Insert the Facebook page url and Customize the Options in 'Facebook Pop-out Likebox' page under the 'Settings' tab
